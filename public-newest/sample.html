RED CROSS YOUTH

National University of Singapore Chapter

Red Cross Youth - National University of Singapore (RCY - NUS) Chapter started our humble origins in 1994, and has since expanded to a range of 8 different projects. Our projects range across various aspects such as humanitarian aid and community service. Members of the RCY will be affiliated to the SRC, one of the largest international humanitarian movement in the world. Register with as a member HERE.
