WHAT WE DO

PROJECT C.L.A.R.E.?

Project C.L.A.R.E is an elderly befriending programme that aims to build up a community resilience within the community at Tampines West area, sharing support such as Eldercare First Aid for Caregiver Class.

OUR STORY

RED CROSS YOUTH - NUS

We are an open chapter, set up for students who are not studying in NUS, previous chapter members who are interested to continue their involvement after graduation, and working adults who are willing to contribute their time to the RCY. Members of the RCY will be affiliated to the SRC, which is member of the largest international humanitarian movement in the world.

    Our activities include:
  • First aid awareness
  • Blood Donation & Donor Recruitment
  • Community Involvement Programme
  • Fundraising
  • International Projects
